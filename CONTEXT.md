# Context

## Canonical Terms

### Mobayilo Academy
The broader education and capacity-building initiative. Its current mission is being refined and executed from Rwanda. Its purpose is to contribute to capacity building by increasing the number of capable software engineers, deepening practical knowledge in computer science, and preparing engineers to solve real local problems.

### AI Coding Cohort Rwanda
A specific current program run by Mobayilo Academy in Rwanda. It is focused on AI-assisted coding and should not be presented as the full definition of Mobayilo Academy.

### Capacity Building
A core framing term for the Academy's mission in Africa. It refers to strengthening the number, capability, and practical effectiveness of software engineers, not just delivering courses or credentials.

### Local Problem Solving
The Academy's primary outcome. Learners are trained to tackle real problems in their own communities and markets. Coding, certificates, engineering knowledge, and product skills are tools used in service of solving those problems.

### Rwanda-First Mission
Mobayilo Academy should present Rwanda as its current focus and operating reality. Collaboration with people in other African countries is part of the long-term direction, but should not be presented as current geographic delivery at the same level as Rwanda.
