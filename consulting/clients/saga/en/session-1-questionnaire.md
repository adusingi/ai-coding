# Session 1 — Audit Questionnaire
**Internal use. Ask these questions verbally in the first 15 minutes of session 1.**
**Goal: understand how the team works today to target the recommendations.**

---

## Block 1 — Current workflow (5 min)

1. When you have a new feature to build, how does it start? Who decides what, how do you divide the work?

2. What do you use to track what needs to be done? (Jira, Notion, GitHub issues, a whiteboard, something else?)

3. When a developer is stuck on something, what do they do? Who do they ask, where do they look?

---

## Block 2 — Current AI usage (5 min)

4. Do you use ChatGPT, Copilot, or anything else today? For what exactly?

5. Does everyone on the team use it, or is it mostly certain people?

6. Has there been a situation where AI gave you something useless or wrong? What happened?

---

## Block 3 — Pain points (5 min)

7. What takes the most time in your team today — something that frustrates you or that you redo often?

8. Is there knowledge or context that only Saga has — things that aren't written down anywhere?

9. If you could change one thing about how you work, what would it be?
